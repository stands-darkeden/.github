<h2>Purpose of DarkEden</h2>
<p><strong>DarkEden</strong> is a free-to-play massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) for Windows, developed by <strong>SOFTON Entertainment</strong>. The game immerses players in a dark fantasy world where humans, vampires, and a hybrid race called Ousters fight for dominance - offering horror, PvP, and faction-based combat in a grim isometric world.</p>

<h2>Main Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Three distinct playable races - Slayers (humans), Vampires, and Ousters - each with unique mechanics and abilities.</li>
  <li>Horror-themed dark fantasy setting: vampiric war in a fictional Eastern European land called Eslania (Helea region), with gothic ambience and lore involving ancient vampires and the vampire "mother" Lilith.</li>
  <li>Isometric / 2D style graphics and classic MMORPG mechanics - hack-and-slay combat, monsters, bosses, quests, loot, and PvP.</li>
  <li>Open-world PvP (player vs player) - outside safe zones, players from different races can fight each other at any time, adding risk, tension, and unpredictability to gameplay.</li>
  <li>Day/night cycle affecting gameplay balance - vampires are stronger at night, while humans (Slayers) and Ousters have advantage during daytime, adding strategic depth.</li>
  <li>Character progression varies by race: Slayers advance via skill system, Vampires need to drink blood after kills to gain full experience, Ousters have different progression mechanics.</li>
  <li>Potential race changes under certain conditions - for example, a human bitten by a vampire may turn into vampire (unless cured in time), adding dynamic race-switch mechanics.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Game Mechanics & Systems</h2>
<ul>

  <li><strong>Race Conversion:</strong> If a human is bitten by a vampire, they risk turning into a vampire unless cured. Vampires may revert human via NPC under certain versions.</li>
  <li><strong>Time-Based Race Balance:</strong> Night boosts vampires, day boosts Slayers/Ousters - influences combat and strategy depending on in-game time.</li>
  <li><strong>Open-World PvP:</strong> Most of the world is PvP-enabled; safe zones are limited. This fosters risk, tension, and player- vs-player conflict.</li>
</ul>
